SDG 14 : Life Below Water

Conserve and sustainably use oceans, seas, and marine resources

Oceans cover over 70% of the Earth's surface and are vital for life.
This project highlights how human activities impact marine ecosystems.

The Problem

  • Plastic Pollution
  • Overfishing
  • Climate Change

Impact on Marine Life

  • Coral Bleaching
  • Loss of Biodiversity
  • Habitat Destruction

Sustainable Actions

  • Reduce Plastic Use
  • Marine Protection Laws
  • Sustainable Fishing
